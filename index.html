Steve And Meredith's Wedding

It's finally here. The most highly anticipated wedding of the century. People of great distinction are traveling from across the world to witness the grand spectacle that is Steve and Meredith's Wedding. There will be laughter. There will be tears. Camp Hoffman

We will have the entire place to ourselves and would love for you to stay with us in a cabin. Come up as early as Saturday morning and stay through breakfast on Sunday. We're happy to provide the roof, firewood, and bug spray – all you need to bring is your sleeping bag and a smile!

Camp Hoffman has comfortable but basic facilities. Think Summer Camp, not 5 star hotel. All beds are singles, each bed has a mattress. Linens are not provided – so you'll need to bring your own. If you're traveling from far away and this is not possible, please let us know and we will provide for you.

There are a few styles of cabins available for our guests. All are clean, dry, and have electricity. There are shared bathroom facilities in or near all cabins and private showers. Campers are welcome to drive their car throughout the camp to drop off belongings.

We will use our best judgment to create cabin assignments, but if you have a special request or conditions for staying at camp, please let us know. There are a few options that are less rustic!

If a private room is a must, the camp experience may not be for you. There are hotels and B&B's in the area. Remember to book early, as it is Memorial Day Weekend.
