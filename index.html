Accomodations

Camp Hoffman

We will have the entire place to ourselves and would love for you to stay in a cabin and spend the whole weekend. Come up as early as Saturday morning and stay through our send-off breakfast on Monday. Join us for swimming and boating on the private lake, sports and games on the field, or just some good old fashioned lounging around. We're happy to provide the roof, meals, firewood, and bug spray – all you need to bring is your sleeping bag and a smile!

Windsor Mountain Camp has comfortable but basic facilities. All beds are singles (no one will have to sleep on a top bunk). Each bed has a mattress, but linens are not provided – so you'll need to bring your own (see packing list below). If you're traveling from far away and this is not possible, please let us know.

There are a few styles of cabins available for our guests, almost all are recently built and all are clean and dry. All have electricity (fans provided) - but be warned, cell reception might be spotty! There are shared bathroom facilities near all cabins and private showers.

We will use our best judgment to create cabin assignments, but if you have a special request or conditions for staying at camp, please let us know.

Feel free to check out the camp website to see more photos of the location and the lodging! www.windsormountain.org

If a private room is a must, the camp experience may not be for you.
